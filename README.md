**Asa Suite: Utils** is a collection of Quality of Life (QoL) utilities for World of Warcraft. It is designed to streamline your gameplay experience by automating tedious tasks and providing quick clean-up tools.

## Features

- **Disable Auto-Add Spells**: Automatically prevents new spells from being added to your action bars when you learn them or change talents.
- **Clear Action Bars**: Quickly wipe all spells and items from your action bars.
- **Clear Quest Log**: Abandon all quests in your quest log with a single command.
- **Expanded Equipment Sets**: Increases the maximum number of equipment sets per player to 100.

## Commands

- `/au` - Opens the configuration menu.
- `/au help` - Displays the help message in-game.
- `/clearbars` - Triggers a confirmation dialogue to clear all spells and items from your action bars.
- `/clearquests` - Triggers a confirmation dialog to abandon all quests in your quest log.
